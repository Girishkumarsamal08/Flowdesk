# Return and Replacement Policy
Customers can return any unworn, unwashed, or defective merchandise within 30 days of the order delivery date for a full refund or exchange.
To request a return, the customer must provide the order number and the reason for the return.
If an item is received wrong or defective, we will ship a replacement at no extra cost immediately. The customer does not need to return the wrong item if it costs less than $50.
Refunds take 3-5 business days to process.
