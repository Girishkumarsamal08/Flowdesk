# Shipping Policy
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days and is free on orders over $50.
Expedited shipping takes 2-3 business days and costs $15.
Overnight shipping costs $25 and is delivered the next business day if ordered before 2 PM EST.
Once an order has shipped, the customer will receive an email with tracking information.
We currently only ship within the United States and Canada.
If a tracking states the package was delivered but the customer cannot find it, they must wait 24 hours before contacting support, as sometimes carriers mark packages delivered early.
